About Dr. Adel Marzban

Physician | Scientist | Entrepreneur | Innovator

My Mission

To bridge the gap between biological innovation and digital transformation in healthcare.

I am Dr. Adel Marzban, a physician, scientist, and entrepreneur with a deep commitment to advancing both tissue engineering and digital health innovation. My journey has always been guided by a single question: how can science and technology work together to restore lives and improve patient care?

Over the past decade, I have built a strong foundation in tissue banking and regenerative medicine. My work includes:

  • Developing acellular dermis membranes for maxillofacial, dental, and wound healing applications.
  • Innovating in allograft processing techniques such as lyophilization, glycerolization, and radiation sterilization.
  • Creating collagen-based biomaterials such as injectable fillers and powders.

But my path does not end with biology alone. I am also deeply engaged in data science and coding, building skills in Python, machine learning, deep learning, SQL/NoSQL, and big data technologies. My vision is to apply these tools in biomedical AI and digital health systems.
