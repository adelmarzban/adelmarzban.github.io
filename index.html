About Adel Marzban

My Mission: Bridging Biology and the Digital Future

I am Adel Marzban, a physician, scientist, and entrepreneur with a deep commitment to advancing both tissue engineering and digital health innovation. My journey has always been guided by a single question: how can science and technology work together to restore lives and improve patient care?

Over the past decade, I have built a strong foundation in tissue banking and regenerative medicine. My work includes developing acellular dermis membranes, innovating in allograft processing techniques, and creating collagen-based biomaterials. As the manager of an allograft product manufacturing company, I have successfully guided the approval and delivery of high-quality tissue products to surgeons.

But my path does not end with biology alone. I am also deeply engaged in data science and coding, building skills in **Python, ML, deep learning, SQL/NoSQL, and big data technologies**.
