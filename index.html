My Mission: Bridging Biology and the Digital Future

"How can science and technology work together to restore lives and improve patient care?"

I am Adel Marzban, a physician, scientist, and entrepreneur with a deep commitment to advancing both tissue engineering and digital health innovation. My journey has always been guided by this single question.

Foundation in Regenerative Medicine

Over the past decade, I have built a strong foundation in tissue banking and regenerative medicine. My work includes developing acellular dermis membranes, innovating allograft processing techniques, and creating collagen-based biomaterials. As the manager of an allograft product manufacturing company, I’ve guided the approval and delivery of high-quality tissue products to surgeons.

Future in Biomedical AI

I’m deeply engaged in data science and coding, building skills in Python, ML, deep learning, SQL/NoSQL, and big data. My vision is to apply these tools in biomedical AI and digital health systems — from predictive modeling to intelligent platforms that support patient monitoring and secure clinical workflows.
