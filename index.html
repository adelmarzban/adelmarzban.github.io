Foundation in Regenerative Medicine

Over the past decade, I have built a strong foundation in tissue banking, allograft processing and regenerative medicine. My work includes establishing allograft processing center, developing allograft products, research and innovating in allograft processing techniques, and creating new ways to faciliate coordination of a tissue bank.
