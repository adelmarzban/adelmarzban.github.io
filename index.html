My Mission: Bridging Biology and the Digital Future

"How can science and technology work together to restore lives and improve patient care?"

I am Adel Marzban, a physician, scientist, and entrepreneur with a deep commitment to advancing both tissue engineering and digital health innovation. My journey has always been guided by this single question.

Foundation in Regenerative Medicine

Over the past decade, I have built a strong foundation in **tissue banking and regenerative medicine**. My work includes developing acellular dermis membranes, innovating in allograft processing techniques, and creating collagen-based biomaterials. As the manager of an allograft product manufacturing company, I have successfully guided the approval and delivery of high-quality tissue products to surgeons, ensuring patients receive the best available options for healing and recovery.
